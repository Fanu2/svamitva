# Registrar Interaction Guide (Sirsa / Kalanwali)

## Purpose
This note records practical, ground-tested guidance on interacting with the
Sub-Registrar office for Agreement to Sell cum Possession involving SVAMITVA properties.

## Key Principles
- Be brief and factual
- Do not volunteer extra information
- Clearly state this is NOT a sale deed

## Accepted Phrases (Hindi)
- "Yeh sirf Agreement to Sell hai, sale deed nahi hai."
- "Sale deed government permission ke baad hogi."
- "Abhi ownership transfer nahi ho rahi."

## Avoid Saying
- Ownership transferred
- Final sale
- Full and final settlement

## Practical Tip
If a junior clerk objects, calmly repeat the above and wait.
Escalation usually resolves the issue.
