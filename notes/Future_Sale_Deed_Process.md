# Future Sale Deed Process (SVAMITVA)

## Expected Flow
1. Seller initiates transfer online (Aadhaar + OTP)
2. Buyer accepts transaction
3. Draft deed auto-generated
4. Stamp duty paid online
5. Both parties appear for biometrics
6. Sale deed registered

## Buyer Preparation
- Ensure agreement data matches portal
- Keep RTGS proofs ready
- Keep seller cooperative and available

## Important
Only the recorded owner (seller) can initiate online transfer.
